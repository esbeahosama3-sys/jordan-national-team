Jordan National Team

The Jordanian football team was founded in 1949, but its official career in the international leagues began after the merger of the Jordanian Federation with the International Football Confederation (FIFA) in 1956. The establishment of its main stadium in the Amman International Stadium, which can accommodate tens of thousands, and the establishment of the respected Jordanian football school has become a strong competitor in the Asian continent today.
